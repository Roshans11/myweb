About This Platform

🏆 What is MyWeb?

MyWeb is an interactive digital platform designed to manage and host competitions, quizzes, or assessments for participants and viewers alike.

👥 Who Can Use It?

Anyone! Participants can log in to take part in contests, while viewers can access public results and updates.

📊 Key Features

  • Easy registration and login
  • Real-time score updates
  • Role-based access: Participant & Viewer
  • Mobile responsive and user-friendly UI

🛠️ Technologies Used

This project uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Future enhancements may include database integration, authentication, and analytics.
